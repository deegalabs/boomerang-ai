Quick guide

How to use it, in practice

Already installed and started the agent? Then you are almost there. With a few taps in Telegram it starts working for you. Here is the best way to use it.

1

Open the bot and send /start

In Telegram, find your bot and send /start. The menu appears with everything you can do. Only you can command it.

2

Configure in 3 choices

Tap Configure and choose: the coin (or "All liquid"), the stop-loss (how much you accept losing) and the take-profit (when to realize the gain). It takes about 30 seconds.

3

Activate

Tap Activate. That is it: the agent starts watching the market and trading on its own, following exactly the rules you set.

4

Follow along

You get a message on every action, with the reason and the result. Want to check anytime? Send /status for the live summary, or /dashboard to open the visual panel.

5

Stay in control

Change your mind anytime: /pause to pause, Withdraw All to send the money back to your wallet, and /panic to exit everything at once in an emergency.

Golden tips

Small habits that make a difference in getting the best from the agent.

🐢

Start small

Use a test bankroll. You learn how the agent behaves without risking much.

🌐

Let it choose

When in doubt, select "All liquid". The agent focuses on the best opportunity of the moment on its own.

⏰

Keep it running

The longer it stays active, the more opportunities it catches. It works 24 hours; you do not have to.

📩

Read the alerts

Each message tells you what it did and why. It is the best way to understand and trust it.
